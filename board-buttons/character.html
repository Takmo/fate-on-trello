Character Skills

Skills describe what your character is good and not-so-good at. When your character engages in activities related to one of these skills, you can include these bonsues on related rolls.

For new characters, Fate Core recommends the following distribution of skills:

  • 1x Great (+4) skill
  • 2x Good (+3) skills
  • 3x Fair (+2) skills
  • 4x Average (+1) skills

Looking for something simpler? Click here to switch to the Fate Accelerated approach system.

Character Approaches

Approaches are adverbs that describe how your character prefers to solve their problems. There are six approaches in all, and a character can use any approach to solve their problem. However, they naturally excel at certain types of approaches. When a character attempts to complete an action using an approach they are skilled with, you can include that bonus on related roles.

For new characters, Fate Accelerated recommends the following distribution of skills:

  • 1x Good (+3) approach
  • 2x Fair (+2) approaches
  • 2x Average (+1) approaches
  • 1x Mediocre (+0) approach

Looking for something more feature-full? Click here to switch to the Fate Core skill system.

Stunts

Stunts describe special ways your character uses skills/approaches to perform an action. Think of these like a signature move your character uses in a fight, or a special skill they have in certain environments.
